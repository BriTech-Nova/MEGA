About Us

Dedicated to youth empowerment since 2023

Our Story

MEGA MOTIVE INC is a government-registered Community-Based Organization (CBO) founded on 15th January 2023 by Education students while in their first year at Maseno University.

After interacting with youths in colleges, high schools, primary schools, and villages, they identified challenges such as financial struggles, relationships, academic pressure, and unemployment.

In response, they developed strategies focusing on goal setting, decision-making, and action-taking to empower the youth.

Mission

To inspire individuals to discover their true potential, make a positive impact on society, and achieve their dreams.

Vision

To create a world where every person is empowered, motivated, and equipped to overcome challenges and lead a fulfilling life.
